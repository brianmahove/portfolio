Bio

I'm Brian Mahove, a versatile and passionate computer science student, developer, and tech innovator dedicated to building real-world solutions through software. With skills in web and mobile development, I’ve created apps ranging from guest management systems to assistive tools for the visually impaired, as well as intelligent agricultural platforms tailored to Zimbabwe’s unique climate.
