Notes App

Organize Your Thoughts and Ideas with Ease

Project Overview

The Notes App allows users to quickly jot down their ideas, thoughts, or important information in a digital format. The app includes features like note creation, categorization, and simple text editing for organizing and storing notes.

Key Features:

  • 📝 Create and edit notes
  • 📂 Organize notes into categories
  • 🔍 Search for specific notes
  • ✏️ Rich text formatting (bold, italics, lists)
  • 🔒 Secure notes with password protection
  • 🗑️ Delete and manage notes

Technologies Used:

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Local Storage for saving notes
